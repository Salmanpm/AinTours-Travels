Kerala Escape

Discover the soul of “God’s Own Country” with our Kerala Escape — a journey that takes you through lush green hills, winding backwaters, spice-scented air, and quiet beaches. This tour is crafted for travelers who want to experience the authentic essence of Kerala in comfort and style.

Start your adventure in Munnar, a misty hill station where endless tea gardens stretch across rolling hills and the cool air rejuvenates your senses. Visit cascading waterfalls like Attukal and Lakkam, walk through aromatic spice plantations, and stay in cozy resorts perched in the heart of nature.

Next, head to the backwaters of Alleppey or Kumarakom, where you’ll cruise through palm-lined canals on a traditional houseboat. Watch life unfold on the banks — fishermen casting nets, women washing clothes, and children waving with joy.

  • Handpicked stays in Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey & beach resort
  • Private AC vehicle with experienced driver
  • Daily breakfast + houseboat lunch & dinner
  • Guided plantation walks and cultural programs
  • Optional activities: Jeep safari, Ayurvedic spa

Wrap up your Kerala experience on the serene beaches of Varkala or Marari, where time slows down and the sunsets are pure poetry. Whether you're trekking through the hills, sipping fresh coconut water, or enjoying an Ayurvedic massage, every moment in Kerala is a celebration of calm, color, and connection.

Come home with memories of foggy mornings, fresh spices, soulful food, and the warmest hospitality — Kerala is a place you’ll carry in your heart forever.
